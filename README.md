# Task 3: Ecosystem Flow

# 1. For Individuals
* Users sign up through app or web to receive a customizable digital business card with a QR code for sharing.
* Cards are exchanged by scanning, linking, or adding contacts within the app.

# 2. For Communities
* Groups use digital cards to connect members and secure funding for green projects.
* Members can grow their network and monitor how many trees their group plants.
* The platform encourages following all 17 UN sustainability goals to show real results.

# 3. For Tree-Planters
* **Tracking**: Planters use the app to log photos, exact locations, and tree types.
* **Offline Access**: The app works both online and offline on mobile phones.
* **Monitoring**: Helps examine the growth and lifecycle of plantations to ensure proper sustainability.
